CardReadResults.php : 21

Class containing results data from the card read service. The data is provided in the form of an array of CardEdgeData objects. Each object contains the information collected for an individual card edge found by the Card Read Service during the card printing process. Please note that multiple card edges may be reported for a given card technology if the card responds to more than one frame protocol (e.g. a MIFARE Classic card supporting ISO14443A and ISO14443A_3).
